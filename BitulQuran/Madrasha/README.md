# Baitul Quran International Madrasha

## 📖 Overview
Baitul Quran International Madrasha is a faith-based educational institution founded in 2015 by Haji Manshurur Rahman Khan (deceased). It is directed by Hafiz Qari Maulana Abdul Basir Nomani and located at Stadium Road, Nandibari, Muktagacha Upazila, Mymensingh, Bangladesh.

---

## ✨ Vision
To integrate **traditional Quranic education** with **modern digital management systems**, ensuring accountability, accessibility, and excellence for both children and adult learners.

---

## 🏫 Departments
- **Nurani Maktab** (basic Quran learning for children)
- **Nazra** (reading fluency)
- **Hifzul Quran** (memorization program)
- **Khatmi/Recitation** (completion and tajweed refinement)

## 🕌 Quran Memorization & Madrasah Module

### 1. **Department Setup**
- **Children’s Section (Tahfiz for Kids)**  
  - Age-based grouping (e.g., 5–7, 8–12)  
  - Daily hifz schedule with short surahs first, gradual progression  
  - Integration with general school timetable (English, Bengali, Arabic subjects)  

- **Adult Section (Tahfiz for Adults)**  
  - Flexible class timings (morning/evening)  
  - Self-paced memorization with teacher supervision  
  - Online/remote participation option for working adults  

---

### 2. **Core Features**
- **Student Profiles**: Track memorization progress, tajweed accuracy, attendance.  
- **Teacher Dashboard**: Assign surahs, monitor recitation, provide feedback.  
- **Assessment System**: Weekly oral tests, monthly completion milestones.  
- **Certification**: Issue digital/printed certificates for completed juz or full Quran.  

---

### 3. **Operational Modules**
- **Curriculum Management**  
  - Structured memorization plan (Juz by Juz)  
  - Tajweed and Qira’at training modules  

- **Residential & Non-Residential Setup**  
  - Hostel management for boarding students  
  - Meal planning, health checkups, library access  

- **Adult Learning Integration**  
  - Evening/weekend schedules  
  - Recorded lectures and revision tools  

---

### 4. **UI Design Plan**
On the **Home Page**, add a dedicated **Quran Memorization Department** section:
- **Admin Panel** → Manage teachers, classes, certification.  
- **Teachers** → Assign surahs, track progress, upload tajweed resources.  
- **Students (Children & Adults)** → Personalized dashboard showing memorization progress, upcoming tests.  
- **Parents/Guardians** → Monitor child’s progress, attendance, and performance.  
- **Community Section** → Events (Khatam ceremonies, competitions).  

Password-based access ensures accountability, with clear policies for evaluation and certification.
